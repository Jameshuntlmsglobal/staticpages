The Market Showdown: Newquay vs. St Ives

While St Ives is known for its art scene, Newquay's vibrant surf culture creates a distinct and resilient market. Newquay often shows higher occupancy, driven by a year-round calendar of events and activities appealing to a younger, more active demographic.

Who Visits Newquay?

Newquay's appeal is broad, but the market is dominated by two key groups: families seeking classic beach holidays and adventure seekers drawn by the world-class surf, creating strong demand for properties that cater to both.

Seasonal Revenue Stream

Revenue peaks dramatically during the summer, supercharged by the Boardmasters Festival in August. Shoulder months (May, June, Sept) remain strong. Astute pricing and marketing can capture valuable bookings during the quieter but still active winter season.
